What is Broken Heart Syndrome?

In technical terms it is when the heart suddenly becomes stunned or weakened. It is often brought about by severe emotional/physical distress, but sometimes it can be triggered by serious physical illness/surgery. Usually it is temporary and can last up to a couple of months, but some do expereince a lingering fatigue.

Broken Heart Syndrome is also called: Stress cardiomyopathy, Takosubo cardiomyopathy, Recurrent takosubo cardiomyopathy, and Apical ballooning syndrom
