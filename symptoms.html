Emotional Stressors

Physical Stressors

High Fever, Stroke, Seizure, Difficulty Breathing, Significant Bleeding, Low Blood Sugar

Symptoms

The symptoms can mimic a heart attack which include: chest pain, shortness of breath, diaphoresis (sweating), dizziness.

Is broken heart syndrome dangerous?

It cal lead to congestive heart failure, low blood pressure, shock , and potentially life threatening heart rhythm abnormalities.
